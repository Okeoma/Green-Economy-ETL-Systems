Title: Business Model Overview and Technical Innovation



Business Model Overview Innovation and Competitive Advantage:
Technological innovation is a key driver in achieving sustainability goals.
Implementing sustainability techniques in big data ETL operations can foster innovation,
such as adopting advanced analytics algorithms, optimizing data storage and processing,
and leveraging renewable energy sources. By embracing technological innovation for sustainability,
SMEs can gain a competitive advantage in the market and position themselves as leaders in the industry.
- The business model encompasses a comprehensive approach to sustainability, driven by technological innovation.
- Key components include user story, acceptance criteria, user journey, process map, website and app development, and the implementation of green economy innovation technology.
