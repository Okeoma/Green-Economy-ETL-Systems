Title: Product Implementation



Website and App Acceptance Criteria - The website and app should provide information on sustainable practices, regulations, technologies, and solutions specifically tailored to SMEs in the ETL sector.
- The platform should offer guidance on reducing energy consumption, optimizing data storage, and adopting renewable energy sources.
- The website and app should include case studies or success stories of SMEs that have successfully implemented sustainable practices in their ETL operations.
- Users should be able to access a resource library with downloadable materials such as guides, whitepapers, and best practice documents.
- The platform should provide tools or calculators to help SMEs measure their carbon footprint, track progress, and set reduction targets.
- Users should have the ability to interact and engage with other SMEs or industry experts through discussion forums or online communities.
- The website and app should have a user-friendly interface, ensuring easy navigation, accessibility, and compatibility across different devices
